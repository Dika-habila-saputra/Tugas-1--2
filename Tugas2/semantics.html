Paris

"Paris" is the capital city of France, located in the northern part of the country. Known as the "City of Light," Paris is famous for its rich history, art, culture, and architecture. Iconic landmarks like the Eiffel Tower, Notre-Dame Cathedral, the Louvre Museum, and the Champs-Élysées attract millions of visitors every year. The city is also renowned for its cuisine, fashion, and role as a global center for business and diplomacy. .

Moscow

Moscow is the capital city of Russia, located in the western part of the country. It is the largest city in Russia and one of the most important cultural, political, and economic centers in the country. Moscow is known for its historic landmarks such as the Kremlin, Red Square, St. Basil's Cathedral, and the Bolshoi Theatre. The city plays a significant role in Russian history and is famous for its stunning architecture, museums, and vibrant arts scene.
