Bandung

"Bandung" is the name of a city located in Indonesia, specifically in West Java province. This city is known by the nickname "City of Flowers" due to its natural beauty and rich history. Bandung is also famous for its cool climate, as a center of education, as well as its popular tourist attractions, such as craters, mountains, and shopping destinations.
